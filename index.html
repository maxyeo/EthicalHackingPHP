The Story

YOU ARE DOM COBB.

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief, the absolute best in the dangerous art of extraction, stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state, when the mind is at its most vulnerable. Your target is Maurice Fischer, head of an energy conglomerate. You must enter multiple levels of dreams and steal his corporate secrets (flags).

YOUR TARGET IS ROBERT FISCHER.

Robert Fischer is the son of Maurice Fischer, the head of a very large energy conglomerate. Robert is slated to inherit the company. You must enter multiple levels of dreams and steal his corporate secrets (flags).
